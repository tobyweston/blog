Bank on Tech Panel Discussion

Deutsche Bank's Bank on Tech event in London, Birmingham, and Dublin brought together technical experts and thought leaders to reflect and learn.

Published: Nov 12, 2025

Deutsche Bank hosted their annual Bank on Tech event in London, Birmingham, and Dublin today where I was fortunate enough to join Kosli in hosting a panel discussion where we discussed amongst other things, the topic of governance and compliance in regulated environments.

The event brought together technical experts, thought leaders and innovators, with over 1.9k attendees, various other interactive panel discussions and over 30 community booths showcasing real examples of how technology is enabling business growth.
